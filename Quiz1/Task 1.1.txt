1. Users can add a new contact with fields including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

2. Users can modify existing contact information, including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

3. Users can remove contacts from the database.

4. Users can search through contacts based on first name, last name, company, job title, phone number, or email address.

5. The system should identify and allow users to merge duplicate contacts, consolidating their information.

6. Each phone number entry should include a label (e.g., home, mobile, work) to specify its purpose.

7. Each email address entry should include a label (e.g., personal, work) to indicate its relation to the contact.

8. The system should allow for zero or more phone numbers and zero or more email addresses per contact.
